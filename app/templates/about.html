About PawsLedger

A universal microchip registry and recovery platform — the single source of truth for pet identification.

Decoupled Identity

Pet records separated from proprietary manufacturer databases. Your data belongs to you, not a chip vendor.

Trusted Transfer

Secure ownership changes with full audit trail. Every handoff is cryptographically recorded.

Seamless Access

Time-bound access for vets, sitters, and caregivers. Share records without sharing your identity.

How It Works

1. Register Your Pet

Add your pet with their 15-digit microchip number, breed, and care details. Link physical NFC or QR tags for instant access.

2. Build the Ledger

Upload vaccination records, set medical alerts, and generate SHA-256 sealed PDF exports for official use.

3. Stay Connected

If your pet is found, finders search the chip or scan the tag. We facilitate safe, anonymous contact through our secure relay.

Industry Standards

PawsLedger integrates with the AAHA Universal Pet Microchip Lookup network and supports ISO 11784/11785 standards for 134.2 kHz FDX-B transponders. All vaccination exports follow NASPHV Form 51 requirements.
