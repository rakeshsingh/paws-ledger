Decentralized Pet Identity

PawsLedger is built on the belief that a pet's identity should not be locked inside proprietary manufacturer databases. We provide a sovereign, cloud-based ledger that links physical microchips to a verifiable digital history.

Our platform empowers owners to manage their pet's records with cryptographic security, ensuring that medical history, ownership proofs, and emergency recovery data remain accessible and immutable for the life of the animal.
