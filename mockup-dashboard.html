Help & FAQ

Answers to commonly asked questions about PawsLedger.

What is PawsLedger? expand_more
PawsLedger is a universal pet microchip registry and recovery platform. We help pet owners register microchips, manage vaccination records, and enable instant identification through NFC/QR tags.
How does the Verified badge work? expand_more
When you subscribe to the Verified or Guardian plan, all your registered pets receive a Verified badge. This badge appears on their public profile and assures finders that the record is actively maintained and contact info is current.
Is my personal information safe? expand_more
Yes. We never expose owner names, addresses, or phone numbers to finders. All communication goes through our secure nudge relay system. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
How do QR and NFC tags work? expand_more
You can link physical QR or NFC tags to your pet's profile. When someone scans the tag (using their phone camera for QR, or tapping for NFC), they see a public pet profile and can send a secure nudge to you if the pet is lost.
Can I transfer ownership of a pet? expand_more
Yes, Verified and Guardian subscribers can initiate secure ownership transfers. The new owner receives an email with a 7-day acceptance link. All transfers are logged in the pet's immutable ledger.
What happens if I cancel my subscription? expand_more
Your pet registrations remain active forever — they're part of the Free tier. However, premium features (alerts, care instructions, document storage, transfers) will be disabled and the Verified badge will be removed.
